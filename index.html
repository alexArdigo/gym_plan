Seated Row
3 × 12 ▾
⚖️ Start: 20–25 kg · Add 5 kg when 3×12 feels easy

Pick whichever is free: Chest-Supported Row → Seated Cable Row

  1. Chest-supported row (best): Lie chest-down on the pad. Pull handles toward you, driving elbows back. No spinal load.
  2. Seated cable row: Sit upright, feet on platform. Pull handle to lower chest. Keep torso still — don't rock.
  3. Both: squeeze shoulder blades together at the end. Hold 1 sec. Return slowly.

  2. Pull dumbbell up toward hip, driving elbow back and up.
  3. Squeeze back at top. Lower slowly. 12 reps each side.

Rest: 60 sec
Face Pulls ⭐
3 × 15 ▾
⚖️ Start: 5–8 kg · Keep it light — form over weight
  1. Cable pulley at face height. Rope attachment. Light weight.
  2. Pull rope toward your face, elbows flared OUT and back.
  3. Hands end at ear level. Hold 1 sec. Torso stays upright.
  4. Return slowly. Repeat.
▶ Watch: Face Pulls Tutorial
  1. Sit at edge of bench, lean forward so chest is near knees.
  2. Hold very light dumbbell in each hand below you.
  3. Raise both arms out to sides until parallel to floor. Squeeze back.
  4. Lower slowly. 15 reps.
▶ Watch: Rear Delt Fly
Rest: 45 sec
Lat Pulldown
3 × 12 ▾
⚖️ Start: 25–30 kg · Add 5 kg when 3×12 feels easy
  1. Sit, grip bar wider than shoulder-width, palms facing away.
  2. Lean back slightly. Pull bar down to upper chest.
  3. Drive elbows down and back. Squeeze back. Hold 1 sec.
  4. Return slowly until arms nearly straight. Repeat.
Rest: 60 sec
▶ Watch: Lat Pulldown Tutorial
Incline Chest Press
3 × 12 ▾
⚖️ Start: 15–20 kg · Add 5 kg when 3×12 feels easy

Pick whichever is free: Incline Chest Press Machine → Flat Chest Press Machine (angle up)

  1. Incline chest press machine: Adjust seat so handles are at upper chest. Back flat on pad, press forward until arms nearly extended.
  2. Flat chest press machine: If no incline machine, use flat press machine — still works chest and shoulders effectively.
  3. Both: elbows at ~45° to body. Return slowly — 3 seconds back. Don't lock elbows.
▶ Watch: Incline Chest Press Machine
  1. Set bench to 30–45° incline. Dumbbell in each hand at chest.
  2. Press up until nearly extended. Elbows at 45° to body.
  3. Lower slowly — 3 seconds down. Repeat.
▶ Watch: Incline Dumbbell Press
Rest: 60 sec

  1. Adjust seat so handles are at shoulder height.

Day B — Lower Body
~45 min · Target: 120–135 bpm during cardio
🚴 Cardio warm-up (any machine)5 min easy
Squat
3 × 12 ▾
⚖️ Start: 20–30 kg (leg press sled) or empty bar (Smith) · Add 10 kg when 3×12 feels easy

Pick whichever machine is free: Leg Press → Hack Squat → Smith Machine

  1. Leg press (safest): Sit in machine, feet hip-width on platform. Press until legs nearly extended, return slowly.
  2. Hack squat machine: Back against pad, feet on platform. Squat down until thighs parallel, drive back up.
  3. Smith machine: Bar on shoulders, feet shoulder-width, toes slightly out. Squat until thighs parallel, drive up.
  4. All options: brace core, drive through heels, squeeze glutes at the top.
▶ Watch: Leg Press Machine ▶ Watch: Smith Machine Squat
  1. Hold one dumbbell at chest height with both hands.
  2. Feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly out.
  3. Sit down between your knees, chest tall, until thighs are parallel.
  4. Drive through heels back up. Squeeze glutes at the top.
▶ Watch: Goblet Squat (last resort)
Rest: 60 sec
Hamstring Curl
3 × 10 ▾
⚖️ Start: 15–20 kg · Add 5 kg when 3×10 feels easy

Pick whichever machine is available.

  1. Seated leg curl: Sit in machine, pad on top of lower legs. Curl legs down. Squeeze at bottom. Lower slowly — 3 sec back.
  2. Standing leg curl: Stand on one leg, curl the other up toward glutes. 10 reps each leg.
  3. Smith machine RDL: Bar at hip height. Hinge forward pushing hips back, lower bar along legs. Drive hips forward to return.
  4. Cable pull-through: Low pulley, rope attachment, face away. Hinge forward, then drive hips forward to return. Squeeze glutes at top.
▶ Watch: Seated Leg Curl ▶ Watch: Standing Leg Curl
  1. Stand, dumbbells in front of thighs, slight knee bend.
  2. Push hips back — not down — lowering dumbbells along your legs.
  3. Keep back flat. Stop when you feel a hamstring stretch.
  4. Drive hips forward to return. Squeeze glutes at top.
▶ Watch: Romanian Deadlift (NASM)
Rest: 60 sec
Glute Bridge
3 × 15 ▾
⚖️ Start: bodyweight or 10–20 kg (machine) · Add 10 kg when 3×15 feels easy

Pick whichever machine is free: Hip Thrust Machine → Smith Machine Hip Thrust

  1. Hip thrust machine: Upper back on pad, feet on platform. Drive hips up, squeeze glutes at top. Hold 1 sec.
  2. Smith machine hip thrust: Upper back on bench under bar. Bar rests on hips with padding. Same movement.
  3. Both: lower slowly — 3 seconds on the way down.
▶ Watch: Hip Thrust Machine ▶ Watch: Smith Machine Hip Thrust
  1. Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat on the floor.
  2. Press lower back into the floor.
  3. Drive through heels, squeeze glutes, lift hips to form a straight line.
  4. Hold 1 sec. Lower slowly. Repeat.
▶ Watch: Glute Bridge Tutorial
Rest: 45 sec
Hip Abduction
3 × 15 ▾
⚖️ Start: 15–20 kg · Add 5 kg when 3×15 feels easy

Hip abduction machine — the one where you sit and push your knees apart.

  1. Sit in the machine, pads on the outside of both knees.
  2. Back flat against pad, core braced.
  3. Push knees apart against the resistance as far as comfortable.
  4. Hold 1 sec at the widest point. Return slowly — 3 seconds back.
▶ Watch: Hip Abduction Machine
  1. Lie on your side, hips stacked, knees bent 45°, feet together.
  2. Keeping feet together, lift top knee up like a clamshell opening.
  3. Hold 1 sec at top. Lower slowly. 15 reps each side.
▶ Watch: Clamshell Proper Form
Rest: 45 sec
Dead Bug
3 × 8 each side ▾
⚖️ Bodyweight only · Never add weight to this exercise
  1. Lie on back, knees bent 90°, feet off floor, arms pointing up.
  2. Press lower back firmly into the mat — keep it there throughout.
  3. Lower right arm behind you + extend left leg simultaneously.
  4. Stop before touching the floor. Return. Switch sides.
Rest: 45 sec
▶ Watch: Dead Bug (NASM)

Leg Press
3 × 10 ▾
⚖️ Start: 30–40 kg · Add 10 kg when 3×10 feels easy

Pick whichever is free: Leg Press → Hack Squat → Smith Machine

  1. Leg press (safest): Feet hip-width on platform. Press until legs nearly extended — don't lock knees. Return slowly.
  2. Hack squat machine: Back on pad, feet on platform. Squat down until thighs parallel, drive back up.

  2. Step right foot fully on bench. Drive through heel to step up.
  3. Step back down. 10 reps each leg.
